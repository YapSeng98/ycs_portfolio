Recurring Billing System

Automates recurring invoice generation with mass Excel upload, configurable billing cycles, multi-level approvals, and real-time SAP integration.

  • Mass upload via Excel template with row-level validation for bulk invoice submission
  • User-defined billing schedules (monthly / quarterly / annual) with auto-generated invoice forms and PDF export
  • Multi-level approval with bulk approve / reject and automated email notifications
  • Real-time SAP sync for GL accounts, cost centres, and internal order data
  • Dashboard with billing status overview, audit trail, and advanced search / filter
  • Role-based access: submitter, approver, and finance admin
