Land Information System

A property management system enabling users to track land parcels, tenancy agreements, building areas, and land fee schedules across all company-owned properties in one centralised platform.

  • Interactive map view displaying locations of all company lands and tenancies
  • Track land area, tenancy details, building specifications, and fee schedules per property
  • Automated lease expiry alerts and renewal notifications sent to property managers
  • Dashboard with reports on occupancy status, rental income, and upcoming expirations
  • Search and filter properties by location, status, and tenancy type
  • Role-based access control for property managers, finance teams, and administrators

Permit to Work

A digital safety permit management system providing a structured, step-by-step process for authorising controlled work in hazardous environments — ensuring compliance, accountability, and full documentation at every stage.

  • Guided digital permit request with work type, hazard declaration, and safety checklist
  • Multi-level approval with safety officer and supervisor sign-off before work commences
  • QR code generated per approved permit — scanned via Now Mobile app for on-site verification
  • Photo capture for work documentation and evidence records at each stage
  • Real-time push notifications and email alerts throughout the approval and closure workflow
  • Permit validity window with automatic expiry and closure enforcement
  • Full audit trail of all permit activities for safety and regulatory compliance

E-Gatepass System

A digital gatepass system for managing and controlling the movement of items in and out of plant premises — replacing manual paper passes with a fully auditable, mobile-enabled approval workflow for enhanced security compliance.

  • Digital gatepass request with item details, purpose, and destination declaration
  • Multi-level approval with department head and security officer sign-off
  • QR code generated per approved gatepass — scanned via Now Mobile app at security checkpoints
  • Photo capture for item documentation at entry and exit points
  • Real-time notifications to security personnel upon approval or rejection
  • Status tracking for requestors from submission through security clearance
  • Full audit log of all gate movements for compliance, security review, and reporting

A centralised material management system for maintaining spare and non-spare parts inventory data, enabling structured creation, amendment, and plant extension requests with multi-level approvals and SAP master data alignment.

  • Custom UI for searching and browsing materials by code, description, and category
  • Create new material records with standardised data entry forms and field validation
  • Amend existing material attributes with tracked change history
  • Extend materials to additional plant codes through a controlled request process
  • Multi-level approval workflow for all create, amend, and extend requests
  • Automated notifications to approvers and requestors at each workflow stage
  • Role-based access control for requestors, approvers, and system administrators
